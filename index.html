Our Approach

Therapy that meets your nervous system first

At Solune, we believe that healing is a process — one that deserves a foundation sturdy enough to hold real weight. We often begin by supporting regulation first — creating enough steadiness for deeper work.

01

Regulation First

Before we explore patterns or build insight, we start by helping your nervous system settle. A sense of safety is something we build together, one session at a time.

02

Evidence-Based

Every approach at Solune is grounded in research — not trend. We draw from validated clinical frameworks to offer care that holds up over time.

03

At Your Pace

Healing does not follow a fixed schedule. Sessions are shaped by what feels present for you, not by a predetermined programme.

Frequently Asked Questions

The 15-minute discovery call is a brief, no-pressure, complimentary conversation. It is designed for us to briefly discuss what is prompting you to seek therapy, answer any practical questions you have about scheduling or fees, and align on whether we are a good fit before booking a session.

Standard therapy sessions are 60 minutes in length. At the outset of our work, meeting weekly is highly recommended to establish consistency, build therapeutic safety, and maintain progress. Over time, as your therapeutic goals are achieved, we may cooperatively transition to bi-weekly or monthly sessions.

class="faq-item">

All sessions are conducted virtually via a secure, confidential video platform. We have found that for professionals managing demanding schedules, telehealth offers the consistency necessary for deep clinical work without the added stress of a commute. Virtual sessions provide the same rigorous, regulation-focused care, allowing you to engage in therapy from a private space where you already feel anchored and comfortable.

We operate a strict 24-hour cancellation policy. Because your session time is reserved exclusively for you, any cancellations, modifications, or rescheduling requests made with less than 24 hours notice will be subject to the full session fee.

Yes, confidentiality is the clinical and ethical foundation of our practice. Everything shared within our sessions is strictly confidential and protected by law. The only rare, legally mandated exceptions involve immediate risk of serious harm to yourself or others, or when required by a direct court subpoena.
